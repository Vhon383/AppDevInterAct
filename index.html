What is Front-End Development?

Front-end development is the art and science of building the visual and interactive elements of websites and applications that users directly engage with. It transforms design concepts into functional, responsive experiences through code. Using languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, front-end developers bridge the gap between static designs and dynamic, user-friendly interfaces that work seamlessly across devices and browsers.
